 No OHIP? No problem...

Our family doctors can assist you even if a) you do not have OHIP coverage, or b) you would like to avail of a service that is not covered by OHIP. We're here for you. We offer a range of non-OHIP services (otherwise known as 'private services'). If you fall under this category, please read the following instructions for how to book with us as the process works a bit differently.

Just follow these steps...

1. Begin by booking an appointment - click on the 'Book Online Now'. Complete your details as prompted and enter the word 'Private' where you are asked for an OHIP number.

2. Pay for your appointment by going back to our homepage and choosing 'Private Services'. There are a range to choose from at the bottom of the page.

3. Select the relevant service. You can pay by interac transfer. credit card or Paypal. Once funds are received, we will confirm safe receipt - and confirm arrangements for the service you've paid for.
