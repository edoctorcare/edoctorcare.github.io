           ...it's easy to install

  1. Search and download the "Pexip Infinity Connect" app for your smartphone
  2. About 5 minutes before your booked video appointment, please ensure that on your smartphone you click the blue 'Start eVisit' button that is featured on the email confirmation you received from Ontario Telemedicine Network
  3. Your smartphone should load up the Pexip app that you have already installed - and you then need to hit the green video call button. 
  4. At this point, if your doctor is not yet on the line, please wait for the doctor to join the call. That's it!
