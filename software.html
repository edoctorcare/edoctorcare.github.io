SOFTWARE REQUIRED

  1. Search and download the "Pexip Infinity Connect" app for your iPhone or iPad (from the App store) - or if you have an Android, search and download the Pexip Infinity Connect app (from the Play store).
  2. If you are struggling to find correct app, the direct link for the Android app is here: https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.pexip.infinityconnect&hl=en_CA and the direct link for the iOS app is here: https://apps.apple.com/ca/app/pexip-infinity-connect/id1195088102
  3. About five minutes before your booked eVisit appointment, please ensure that on your smartphone you click the blue 'Start eVisit' button that is featured on the email confirmation you received from Ontario Telemedicine Network
  4. Your smartphone should load up the Pexip app that you have already installed - and you then need to hit the green video call button. At this point, if your doctor has not yet joined the video call (possibly because your doctor is delayed having seen patients prior to you), please wait for your doctor to join the call. Alternatively, your doctor may already be on the line, in which case, you can begin the video consultation immediately.
