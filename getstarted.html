  1. In the first instance, we ask that you please consider whether you are comfortable having an "eVisit" video conference to discuss your medical issue with Dr Tawfik. If you decide you'd rather see Dr Tawfik in person, this option still exists and is available to you via the normal channels. 
  2. Please ensure you are discussing only one issue per consultation as this will help Dr Tawfik to be punctual for your appointment.
  3. Appointments for eVisits are booked online only (more details on how to book are below). Only one slot per day must be booked.
  4. Once you have booked an eVisit appointment slot online, you will get an initial email confirming your slot booking. Within 24 hours of this first confirmation email, you will then receive a second email confirmation from Ontario Telemedicine Network (the host provider of the video call service) confirming again the date / time of your appointment, together with details on how to connect at the time of your appointment.
  5. Please remember to put the date and time of your eVisit in your personal diary as well. Dr Tawfik reserves the right to charge a $50 cancellation fee should you book an eVisit and you do not attend your eVisit appointment. 
  6. Please note that that there are rules governing this service and that Dr Tawfik is only allowed to provide family doctor services to patients who are already enrolled with Dr Tawfik.
  7. Please note that any prescriptions for you that Dr Tawfik elects to issue on the back of the video consultation will be faxed (or emailed, if you consent for email to be used) to the relevant pharmacy.
