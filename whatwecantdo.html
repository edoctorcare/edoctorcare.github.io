A few things we can't help out with...

If you are too unwell to wait for your appointment, or if this is an emergency, please attend your local ER rather than booking an appointment with us. We are always happy to provide you with advice if you are unsure, but this should not delay you seeking emergency care if you are too unwell. Also, we do not prescribe controlled drugs on our platform.


Special note regarding medical exemptions for Covid19 Vaccine:

Please be aware that there are very few acceptable medical exemptions to the COVID-19 vaccination. The only valid exemptions are for people with documented severe allergies to ingredients in the vaccine or those who experienced severe allergic reactions or heart inflammation after their first dose. Given the rarity of these exceptions, and in light of the fact that vaccines have been proven to be both safe and effective, many requests for medical exemptions will be denied.
